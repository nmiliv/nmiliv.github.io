Travling

My perception of the world is mostly shaped by what I eat and who I eat with. While this serves as a list of cool (and uncool) places I’ve been while travelling and other cool things I’ve seen, you’ll notice that this is mostly a list of cool places I’ve eaten at. Unfortunately, this list is far from objective. The same way a single thread cannot be separated from a fabric without fundamentally changing the role of the thread and the structure of the fabric, neither can a single event or aspect of an event be disentangled from the greater fabric of life. Good company makes mediocre food fantastic; even the most exquisite food cannot distract from an argument at the table. Much of my perception of these places is warped by the people I was with when I visited.

If you’ve been to any of these locations, let me know what you thought! If you feel as though I have grossly misrepresented or am missing some context that I should add somewhere, please reach out to me and I will do my best to fix things. I know how picky I can get around my hometown, and I want to show that same respect to everyone’s hometowns :)
